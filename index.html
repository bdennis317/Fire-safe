The Problem

Wildfire risk is rising. Most homeowners are flying blind.

Insurance cancellations are surging, regulations are tightening, and there's no easy way to know where you stand — or what to do about it.

📋

Your insurance is at risk

Carriers are dropping homeowners across California and the West. If you can't prove your property meets defensible space standards, you could face non-renewal — with nowhere else to turn.

🔍

Nobody tells you what to fix

You've probably heard of "defensible space" but have no idea if your yard complies — or what the zones even mean. The rules are confusing, and help is hard to find.

⏳

You can't wait for an inspection

Fire agencies are overwhelmed — thousands of homes, not enough inspectors. You could wait years for someone to assess your property. That's time you don't have.

How It Works

Your fire readiness report in three steps

No inspectors, no appointments, no waiting. Snap a few photos, and our AI combines them with satellite imagery to assess your property in minutes.

1

Enter your address

Type in your street address. We pull up your property using public parcel data and satellite imagery to start your assessment.

2

Snap photos of your home
